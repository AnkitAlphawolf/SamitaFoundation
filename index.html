Samita Foundation was established on 26th December 2016, by its founder Mrs. Samita Singh. It's located at Shahpur Danapur Cantt Patna Bihar. It is an NGO that promotes education for Girls and empowers them with education. It has a placement facility for the students after the completion of courses. This institute provides skills needed in the 21st era Such as Computer Basic Knowledge, Personality grooming, and Language courses. There are bachelor courses are also held with the affiliation of Maulana Mazharul Haque Arabic and Persian University.
